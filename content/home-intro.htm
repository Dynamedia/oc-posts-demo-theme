The Posts plugin for October CMS (and compatible derivatives) has been designed to make running a content-rich website really easy. No matter what size your project, Dynamedia Posts will simplify your workflow.

Creating content is extremely simple and the plugin has been designed with the intention that anybody can use it. Theme developers can provide multiple layouts and post structures, so end users can just get on with writing.

Administrators can make use of over 20 distinct permissions to create roles for writers, editors and publishers. You can have confidence that your website is safe no matter who is writing for you.
